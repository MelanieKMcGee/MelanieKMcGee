# Mel McGee

AI Engineer. AI Strategist. Innovator. Entrepreneur. Nearly 30 years
building software and running companies — and I've been building AI
systems since before "AI" was a category anyone talked about.

Both of my companies are AI Native. Not a chatbot bolted onto the side —
AI runs as a working layer across sales, delivery, finance, and strategy,
at both organizations. I built it that way for myself first.

In 2024, I founded SkillSpout, an award-winning AI adoption company,
where I'm Co-founder and CEO. We help other businesses and organizations
get AI Native too — human-first — through strategy, training, and
build/development.

In 2014, I founded We Can Code IT, an award-winning coding bootcamp built
for people the traditional tech pipeline overlooks — career changers,
self-taught engineers, people from non-traditional backgrounds. I'm still
Founder and CEO.

I started building AI before it had a name. In 1997 I founded my first
company, imageNation Web Experts, where I built a wearable computer by
hand — a PC in a bag, a one-handed keyboard, a homemade heads-up display
— and two of the earlier conversational AI agents I know of, Zoe and
Skeletor. That work was featured in Howard Rheingold's *Smart Mobs*. My
early web innovations were also featured in Curt Harler's *Beyond the
Browser*.

I was an adjunct professor teaching Python, SQL, and data science at the
Weatherhead School of Management (Case Western Reserve University), and
a former CIO at HISC (Healthcare). I've taught throughout my career as a
developer — including at colleges — and that's what grew into founding
We Can Code IT. I also serve as CTO of the Korda Institute for Teaching.
